About the project

The outbreak of infectious diseases, meaning, their appearance in regions not previously affected, requires the scientific community to produce knowledge on a large scale in order to apply measures for rapid control of the disease.

The dissemination of knowledge to the population becomes a crucial factor in the course of a pandemic, since strategies to control the pathogen depend on the entire population.

The importance of scientific dissemination becomes allied in combating the spread of the disease, becoming an indispensable tool as a guide for health managers and sanitarians.

In this line of thought, mathematical modeling in epidemiology stands out as a protagonist in the assessment of the dynamics of the disease and the reproduction of scenarios that envision alternatives for control.

Follow our newsletter where we present the reasons for the importance of developing an Interactive Platform for evaluating scenarios and mathematical models applied to the new Sars-CoV2 coronavirus.
